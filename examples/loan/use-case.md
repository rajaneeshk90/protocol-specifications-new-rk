# Solar Panel Installation Loan - Use Case

## User Journey

1. **Discover**: Customer searches for loans for solar panel installation. To search, customer provides details of the proposal document they got from a solar panel installation provider.

2. **on_Discover**: Customer gets a list of loan offerings based on the provided details, from multiple loan providers, mostly banks.

3. **Application**: Customer chooses one loan offering and applies for it, providing the customer and solar panel installation service provider details (as the loan will be on customer's name but will be dispersed to the installation company account).

4. **Disbursement**: The loan is dispersed in multiple parts to the solar panel seller account.

5. **Status Updates**: Customer gets status and also the update when a part is disbursed to the vendor account.
